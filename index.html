Draw something interesting.



Need help? We'll give you a push. Here's some information on what the buttons do.

Start Recording - Starts the recording.

Stop Recording - Stops the recording.

Save Recording - Saves the recording.

Play Recording - Plays your recording!

Clear - Clears the drawing board and any unsaved recordings made.

Change Color - Changes your drawing color, based on hue.

Retrieve Saved Recording - Retrieves your saved recording. Click "Play Recording" to watch your drawing come to life!
